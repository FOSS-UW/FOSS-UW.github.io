Sep 27 2021

First club meeting of Fall 2021

The Free and Open Source Software club will be having its first meeting of the 2021-22 school year this Thursday, Sept. 30, in room 1221 in the Computer Sciences building at 5:00PM!

If you're interested in joining the club and becoming a member of a group of students who are passionate about free and open source software, please stop by! It'll be a great way to meet other members of the club and learn more about us.

If you haven't already, please sign up for our newsletter, and join us on discord and github in order to stay up to date with all of the club's meetings and projects.

We hope to see everyone there, and we look forward to meeting all of you! We have an exciting year planned for the Free and Open Source Software Club here at UW-Madison and we hope you can join us and be a part of it!
